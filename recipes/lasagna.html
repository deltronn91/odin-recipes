My lasagna recipe

Recipe Description

To make lasagna you need several ingredients. The ingredients should be vegan meat, , make sure the vegan meat is lean.
Next, we want onion and garlic. Make sure to include at least 2 cloves of garlic cooked with veggie meat.
We also want to add tomato and lasagna noodles. Find whatever noodles or tomatoes satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • vegan meat
  • ogranic and garlic
  • tomato products
  • sugar
  • spices and seasonings
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. make the vegan meat sauce
  2. cook the noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
