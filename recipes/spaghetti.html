My Spaghetti Recipe

Recipe Description

Making spaghetti is not hard at all. You first need a few basic ingredients like noodles that are strong and thick. You'll also want to make sure you select tomoato sauce that packs a punch. Lastly, meat is optional. If you're anything like me, though you'll probably want vegan meat.

ingredients

  • 1 lb vegan ground beef (optional)
  • 1 medium onion, and bell pepper chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 ounce of diced tomato
  • 6 ounces of tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt

STEPS

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. combine meat, garlic, and peppers
  3. cook unitl meat is brown and crumbly
  4. add tomato sauce and stir
  5. serve hot and enjoy
